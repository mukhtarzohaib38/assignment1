About Pakistan

Pakistan is a country located in South Asia, bordered by India to the east, Afghanistan and Iran to the west, and China to the north. It has a rich cultural heritage and is known for its diverse landscapes, from the towering peaks of the Himalayas to the vast deserts of Thar. The country boasts a variety of languages, with Urdu being the national language and English widely used in business and government. Pakistan's history is marked by ancient civilizations, such as the Indus Valley Civilization, and it plays a significant role in regional geopolitics.
